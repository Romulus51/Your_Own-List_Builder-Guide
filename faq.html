FAQ

Questions, answered directly

Ownership & Trust
Do you have access to my roster data or my Sheet? +

No. Your Sheet lives in your own Google Drive, and your Apps Script deployment runs under your own Google account. I never see, store, or have access to any of it. The app itself, once you've hosted it, talks directly between your browser and your own Google account — there's no third server in between.

What happens if you stop supporting this, or take the page down? +

Nothing changes for you. You're hosting your own copy of the app, on your own GitHub and Cloudflare account, connected to your own Sheet. Once it's set up, it doesn't depend on me, this website, or any service I run staying online. That's the whole point of building it this way.

The Apps Script deployment setting says "Who has access: Anyone" — is that safe? +

Yes. This setting controls who can call the script's web address, not who can browse your Drive or edit your Sheet. The URL itself is long and effectively unguessable, and the script only exposes the specific read-only actions written into it — there's no way to reach anything else in your Google account through it. This is the standard way Apps Script web apps are deployed.

Can I edit the app's code myself? +

Yes — it's a single, readable HTML file, and once you've bought it, it's yours to modify however you like. I can't promise support for heavily modified versions, but there's nothing locking you out of the code.

Setup & Technical
I'm not very technical. Can I actually do this? +

If you can follow a recipe, you can follow the setup guide — it's the same handful of steps every time, with a screenshot for each one. It's worth reading through it before buying anything, specifically to check this for yourself: everything except the final app file is free to look at first.

Will I hit any usage limits? +

Google's free Apps Script tier allows around 20,000 requests a day and roughly 90 minutes of total script runtime per day, per Google account. For one person using their own list builder for their own games, this is far more headroom than you'll ever use — these limits matter for someone trying to serve hundreds of people from a single account, not for personal use.

Do I need to know how to code? +

No. The Apps Script step involves pasting in a complete, ready-to-use script — you're not writing anything from scratch. The same goes for the Supabase setup script, if you choose to use that optional step.

Can I update the card data later — new units, errata, etc.? +

Yes, any time. Just edit your Google Sheet directly — add a row, fix a typo, update a stat — and refresh the app. There's no redeployment needed for data changes, only for changes to the app's code itself.

What if I lose my Apps Script URL or Supabase keys? +

You can always retrieve them again from your own Apps Script and Supabase dashboards — nothing is single-use or expires. You can also update them at any time from Settings → Edit Connections inside the app, without needing to re-host anything.

Pricing & Purchase
What exactly do I get when I pay? +

The app's four files: index.html (the app itself), sw.js (service worker for offline resilience), apple-touch-icon.png (App icon on mobile) and manifest.webmanifest (home screen install). The Apps Script connectors (Code.gs & appsscript.json), the Supabase table script, and this entire guide are all free — no purchase required to access them.

Is this a subscription? +

No. It's a one-time purchase. There's no recurring charge, ever, for any part of this setup — Google Sheets, Apps Script, GitHub, Cloudflare, and the free tier of Supabase all stay free for personal use at this scale.

Do I get future updates? +

Yes — updates to the app file are included. Since it's a single file, updating just means downloading the new version and re-uploading it to your GitHub private repository (Cloudflare automatically updates); your Sheet, your connections, and your saved rosters are untouched by an update.

About the Project
Is this affiliated with Mantic Games or 343 Industries? +

No — this is an independent, unofficial fan project, built by a player for other players. It isn't endorsed by, sponsored by, or affiliated with Mantic Games, 343 Industries, or Microsoft.

Does it actually work offline? +

Sort of — and it's worth knowing exactly what that means. Load the app once while you have signal, and it'll keep working for the rest of that session even if you lose connection: browsing, building, the works. The catch is it only lasts for that session — closing the app or reloading the page while offline means you'll need signal again to reopen it, since the card database isn't saved to your device by default. There's a Settings option to preload every card image in advance too, so browsing doesn't depend on each image loading on demand. Think of it as "resilient to a dropped connection mid-session," not "fully usable with no signal from a cold start."

Should I still consider the official Mantic app? +

If you play in ranked events or care about Global Rankings, yes — those aren't included on our side, and Mantic's official app ($2.99/mo) is the real way to get them. Worth knowing: Mantic's free tier already covers full rules and one list at no cost, so it's worth checking whether you actually need the paid tier before assuming you do. This tool is built for casual players who want a great list builder without an ongoing bill.
