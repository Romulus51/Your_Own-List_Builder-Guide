Why This Exists

You're not a tournament player. Why pay like one?

Mantic's official app is genuinely good if you're running ranked events and need Global Rankings. But if you just play casually — a game here, a game there, no events — a recurring subscription for an army list doesn't make sense. This is built for that second kind of player.

Mantic Official AppThis List Builder
Cost $6.20/mo (one game) or $10.30/mo (all games), billed monthly One-time purchase, pay what you want

Both can coexist
This isn't trying to replace the official app for everyone — if you run tournaments or care about Global Rankings, you still want that. This is the option for the rest of us: casual players who just want a great list builder without an ongoing bill.
How It Works

Three pieces, all under your control

Nothing about this setup depends on a server run by anyone but you.

📊

1. Your Google Sheet

Free, and you can copy it in one click. This is your card database — every character, weapon, and rule lives here, editable by you at any time.

⚙️

2. Your Apps Script

A small free Google service that connects your Sheet to the app. Deployed under your own Google account — nobody else touches it.

💻

3. The App Itself

A single file you host for free on GitHub Pages or Cloudflare Pages. This is the paid part — everything else above is free and explained in full in the setup guide.
