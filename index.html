Why This Exists

You're not a tournament player. Why pay like one?

Mantic's official app is genuinely good if you're running ranked events and need Global Rankings. But if you just play casually — a game here, a game there, no events — a recurring subscription for an army list doesn't make sense. This is built for that second kind of player.

Custom house rules / point limits Fixed formats only Fully custom ruleset builder, shareable with friends

Both can coexist
This isn't trying to replace the official app for everyone — if you run tournaments or care about Global Rankings, you still want that. This is the option for the rest of us: casual players who just want a great list builder without an ongoing bill. Mantic's own free tier is also worth knowing about — it already includes the full rules and one list builder list at no cost, with the subscription mainly unlocking unlimited lists, ranked play, and Global Rankings.
The actual math
The suggested price on Itch ($12) is less than 4 months of Mantic's $2.99/mo subscription. After that, this is free forever — no renewal, no price changes, nothing lost if you ever miss a payment. At the $6 minimum, you're past break-even in about 2 months.
How It Works

Three pieces, all under your control

Nothing about this setup depends on a server run by anyone but you.

📊

1. Your Google Sheet

Free, and you can copy it in one click. This is your card database — every character, weapon, and rule lives here, editable by you at any time.

⚙️

2. Your Apps Script

A small free Google service that connects your Sheet to the app. Deployed under your own Google account — nobody else touches it.

💻

3. The App Itself

A small set of files you host for free using GitHub (private repo) and Cloudflare. This is the paid part — everything else above is free and explained in full in the setup guide.
